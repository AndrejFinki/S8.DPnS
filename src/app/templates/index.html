How to add image for processing

1. Click on the "Add Image" link in the navigation bar.

2. Select the image you want to add from your device.

3. Click the "Upload" button to add the image.

How to view images after processing

1. Click on the "List Images" link in the navigation bar.

2. You will see a list of images that have been processed.

3. Click on any image to view it in detail.
